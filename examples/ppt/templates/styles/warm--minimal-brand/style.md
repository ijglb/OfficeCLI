# Minimal Brand — Warm Minimalist Branding

## Style Overview
A warm minimalist design system featuring organic ellipse decorations and a refined brown-cream color palette. Perfect for premium brand presentations that balance modern simplicity with approachable warmth.

- **Scenario**: Brand introductions, product launches, premium brand showcases
- **Mood**: Warm, refined, minimalist, premium
- **Tone**: Warm cream, brown tones

## Color Palette
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Background | #F9F6F0 | Warm cream canvas |
| Decoration | #F3EFE6 | Warm white for ellipse backgrounds |
| Primary text | #2B2624 | Dark brown for headings |
| Secondary text | #8B6F47 | Medium brown for body text |
| Accent | #C2A878 | Light golden brown for highlights |

## Design Techniques
- Large ellipse background decoration for organic visual interest
- Minimalist brown color scheme for premium brand feel
- Warm cream canvas with organic shapes
- Card-based pillar layout with rounded rectangles
- Asymmetric evidence layout with large numbers
- Clean typography hierarchy with generous spacing
