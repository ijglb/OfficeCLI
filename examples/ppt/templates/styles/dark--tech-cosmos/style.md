# Tech Cosmos — Scientific Blue-Purple

## Style Overview
Scientific and futuristic design with cosmic deep blue background and blue-purple dual accent system. Features geometric circle and slash scene actors with comprehensive ghost mechanism, ideal for technical and architectural presentations.

- **Scenario**: Tech talks, architecture reviews, scientific presentations, team training, developer conferences
- **Mood**: Futuristic, scientific, mysterious, cosmic
- **Tone**: Deep dark blue with blue and purple accents

## Design Techniques
- Circle and slash scene actors
- Line and box geometric decoration
- Comprehensive ghost mechanism with opacity=0
- Timeline slide type for process visualization
- Blue-purple dual accent system
- 6-slide structure with rich content types
